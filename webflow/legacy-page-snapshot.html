What you can calculate

More than 50 calculators across eleven categories. Geometry and volume: rectangle, triangle and circle area, circumference, and tank volumes (rectangular, cylinder, cone/hopper, sphere). Flow and pressure: gpm to MGD, detention time, velocity, pressure and head, and lift-station cycle time. Treatment and filtration: CT disinfection with a compliance ratio, and filtration/backwash rate. Process control: F/M ratio, MCRT and sludge age (SRT), return (RAS) rate, and recirculation ratio. Solids and settling: sludge volume index (SVI), suspended and total solids, percent removal, and volatile solids. Wells and distribution: well drawdown and specific capacity, water-loss summary, and water use (gpcd). Dosage and chemical: chlorine dosage, chemical feed rate, dilution, and concentration. Pumps and power: brake, water, and motor horsepower, wire-to-water efficiency, Ohm's law, and electrical power. Lab: alkalinity, hardness, and BOD. Plus length, area, volume, weight, and flow unit conversions and specific gravity.
